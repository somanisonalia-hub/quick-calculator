# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: January 6, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Quick Calculator ("we," "our," or "us"). This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website to enhance your experience and provide you with personalized content.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, analyze website usage, and improve our services.

## 3. How We Use Cookies

### 3.1 Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:
- **Session Cookies**: Help you navigate the website and use its features
- **Authentication Cookies**: Keep you logged in during your session
- **Security Cookies**: Protect against security threats

### 3.2 Analytics Cookies
We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website:
- **Usage Analysis**: Track page views, time spent, and user interactions
- **Performance Monitoring**: Monitor website performance and identify issues
- **Improvement Insights**: Help us improve user experience

### 3.3 Functional Cookies
These cookies enhance your browsing experience:
- **Language Preferences**: Remember your language selection
- **Calculator Settings**: Save your calculator preferences and inputs
- **User Interface**: Remember your display preferences

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

### 4.1 Google Analytics
- **Purpose**: Website analytics and performance monitoring
- **Data Collected**: Anonymous usage statistics
- **Privacy**: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
- **Opt-out**: Available through Google's opt-out browser add-on

### 4.2 Social Media Plugins
- **Purpose**: Social sharing functionality
- **Data Collected**: Only when you interact with social buttons
- **Privacy**: Subject to respective social media platform policies

## 5. Cookie Management

### 5.1 Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Block All Cookies**: May limit website functionality
- **Delete Existing Cookies**: Remove stored cookie data
- **Cookie Exceptions**: Allow cookies for specific websites

### 5.2 Our Cookie Preferences
- **Cookie Banner**: Accept or reject non-essential cookies
- **Settings Panel**: Modify your cookie preferences anytime
- **Opt-out Links**: Provided for third-party services

## 6. Data Retention

- **Essential Cookies**: Stored for the duration of your session or as needed
- **Analytics Cookies**: Typically stored for 26 months
- **Functional Cookies**: Stored for up to 2 years
- **Regular Review**: Cookie usage reviewed and updated regularly

## 7. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have rights regarding cookies:
- **Right to Information**: Clear information about cookie usage
- **Right to Consent**: Control over non-essential cookies
- **Right to Withdraw**: Change preferences at any time
- **Right to Object**: Object to certain types of cookies

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect:
- Changes in our cookie usage
- New legal requirements
- Technological advancements
- User feedback
